About Zeppelin Computing

A consultancy built on practical expertise and a commitment to getting things right, without succumbing to the needless complexity and obfuscation which plagues the tech and consulting industries today.

Who We Are

Zeppelin Computing is an IT consulting firm that works with primarily small businesses to solve real technology problems without costing an arm and a leg. We combine technical insight with a straightforward, low-complexity approach. After all, the more moving parts a system has, the more thoroughly it breaks.

Murphy's Law comes for us all, and it's best to have the least amount of things that can go wrong when it does. This philosophy is what has allowed all the most stable, reliable technologies in history to operate dependably.

Whether you need help with a single project or long-term technology guidance, we're here to deliver results, not jargon.

Our Approach

We believe good IT consulting starts with listening. Every organization is different, and cookie-cutter solutions rarely hold up. We take the time to understand your environment before recommending a path forward.

Our goal is simple: leave your technology in a better state than we found it, and make sure your team understands what we've done and why. If you have IT staff, they should be able to take over a task or project of ours with relative ease - that's the goal!

Most importantly, we work to make sure our solutions fit within your budgets, not the other way around!
